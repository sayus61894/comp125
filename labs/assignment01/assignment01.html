Welcome to the Basal Metabolic Rate Calculator

The basal metabolic rate is a measure of the rate at which the body burns energy. This is usually stated in terms of time. One method of estimating the metabolic rate of a person is using the Harris-Benedict Equation. It takes into account the intensity of a person's activity, gender, weight, height, and their age. Over the years there have been many improvements to the equation; here we will be using the original Harris-Benedict Equation.
